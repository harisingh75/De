De Lounge Cafe

A Perfect Destination to Relax & Indulge

De Lounge Cafe is Janakpur Dham's go-to destination for those who appreciate great ambience, quality food, and a relaxed pace of life. Located on the ground floor of Hotel 7 Star at Bajrang Chowk, our cafe combines stylish interiors with a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

What sets De Lounge apart is our attention to detail — from the charming decor and perfect lighting to the soothing music that plays in the background. Guests love our outdoor seating area, which offers a pleasant place to unwind while enjoying the energy of Janakpur's streets.

Our menu caters to diverse tastes — whether you're craving crispy veg snacks, a hearty chicken curry, comforting noodles, or a rich dessert to end your meal. We're proud to offer an English-language menu, making us a favourite among tourists and foreign visitors to Janakpur.

From university students and solo diners to family groups and tourists — De Lounge is Janakpur's most versatile and welcoming cafe lounge.
